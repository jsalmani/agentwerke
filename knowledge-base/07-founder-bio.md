# About the Founder

Agentwerke is founded by Jason, based in Chapel Hill, NC.

## Background

Jason spent the last decade as a sales engineer and solutions architect at the intersection of enterprise automation and content systems. He led the global sales engineering team at Xillio, working with enterprise clients on content migration and ECM integrations. Before that, he worked extensively with RPA platforms — Blue Prism, UiPath, and C TWO Intelligent Automation Orchestration — designing and deploying automated workflows for clients including DHL Global Business Services and McDonald's.

That background matters because building agents for SMBs is a different problem from building chatbots. Office work isn't about answering questions — it's about getting things done across multiple systems with judgment. That's automation work, and Jason has spent his career on it.

## Why Agentwerke

In late 2025 it became clear that Claude had crossed a threshold. The same workflows that took weeks of bot development on traditional RPA platforms could now be built in days using Claude with good tool integration. The technology had caught up to the ambition — and the small businesses that could most benefit from it were the ones least likely to have the technical team to build it themselves.

Agentwerke exists to be that team. Not for Fortune 500s with $500K AI budgets and dedicated procurement processes, but for the law firm with 12 attorneys, the HVAC company with 35 employees, the property management firm with three locations. The businesses where one good agent makes a measurable difference.

## What Jason brings to your engagement

- Direct, hands-on technical work. There's no junior implementer behind the scenes. The person you talk with in discovery is the person who builds your agent.
- A bias toward shipping. Two-week builds, not two-month consulting engagements.
- Honesty about scope. If an agent isn't the right tool for what you're trying to do, we'll say so.
- Local presence. Triangle clients get in-person discovery and quarterly business reviews. Remote clients get the same level of engagement over video.
