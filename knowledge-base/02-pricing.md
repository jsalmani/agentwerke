# Pricing

Agentwerke uses transparent, productized pricing. No "contact us for a quote" gating. Three tiers, fixed scope, predictable cost.

## The three tiers

### Assistant Lite — $6,000 setup + $500/month

For solo professionals or two-person operations who need a single agent doing a focused job.

- One agent, one primary use case (scheduling, FAQ answering, intake routing, or similar)
- One system integration (e.g., calendar OR email OR CRM — pick one)
- A small knowledge base (typically 10-30 pages of documents)
- Up to 2,000 agent interactions per month
- Email support, 24-hour business-day response

### Office Manager Agent — $12,000 setup + $1,000/month

Our most common engagement. Designed for small businesses with 5-50 employees who would otherwise hire a part-time office manager or executive assistant.

- One Office Manager Agent with a multi-skill workflow
- Up to three system integrations (typical: calendar + email + CRM)
- Knowledge base of your services, pricing, FAQs, and procedures
- Multi-step workflow automations (e.g., new-client intake across three systems)
- Up to 10,000 interactions per month
- Monthly optimization session — we review what the agent is doing and improve it
- Quarterly business review
- Email + Slack support, business-day response, 4-hour for critical issues

### Operations Suite — $20,000-$30,000 setup + $2,000-$2,500/month

For mature small businesses with multiple departments or locations who want an agent that operates across several functions.

- Office Manager Agent + additional specialized agents (e.g., sales, support, internal knowledge)
- Five or more integrations
- Custom workflows including invoicing prep, vendor reconciliation, multi-location reporting
- Voice channel optional
- Dedicated monthly improvement sprint
- On-call retainer hours included
- Priority support, 4-hour business-day response

## Add-ons

- **Voice channel** — $300-$500/month
- **Additional integration** — $1,500-$3,000 one-time per system
- **Custom workflow** — $2,000-$5,000 one-time per workflow
- **Higher interaction quota** — $0.05-$0.15 per interaction over your tier's quota

## How this compares to your alternatives

Most of our clients evaluate us against hiring an in-house office manager or executive assistant. Here's how the numbers work in our market (Triangle, NC):

- A full-time Office Manager in Raleigh-Durham runs $50,000-$65,000 in base salary, or $65,000-$85,000 fully loaded once you add benefits, payroll tax, and PTO.
- A full-time Executive Assistant runs $90,000-$110,000 fully loaded.
- A premium dedicated virtual assistant from a service like Belay or Boldly runs $30,000-$45,000 per year for part-time, $50,000-$65,000 for full-time.

Our Office Manager Agent at $12,000 setup plus $12,000 in retainer fees comes to **$24,000 in year one and $12,000 every year after** — roughly one-third the cost of an in-house office manager, and well below what a premium virtual assistant service charges.

The agent doesn't take sick days, doesn't turn over, and doesn't need a 90-day ramp.

## What's not included

- Direct labor for tasks the agent cannot do (we don't replace your team — we extend them)
- Major scope changes after the build (handled as add-ons or a new SOW)
- Custom integrations to systems we haven't worked with before (we'll quote these as add-ons)
- API costs to third parties we integrate with (e.g., your CRM's API costs are yours)

## Payment terms

- Setup fee: 50% on signed SOW, 50% on go-live
- Monthly retainer: invoiced first of each month
- 30-day notice to cancel month-to-month after the initial 90-day commitment
