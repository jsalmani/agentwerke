# Pricing

Agentwerke uses transparent, productized pricing for broker-dealer engagements. No "contact us for a quote" gating. Three tiers, fixed scope, predictable cost.

## The three tiers

### Onboarding Accelerator — $25,000 setup + $2,000/month

For BDs that recruit 20+ reps per year and want to take the operational drag out of onboarding.

- New rep onboarding agent (U4, OBAs, state registrations, bridge license workflow)
- Branch office paperwork automation
- Integration with your CRM, document management, and FINRA Web CRD
- Up to 5,000 agent interactions per month
- 8-week build
- 12-month minimum term
- Monthly optimization session and quarterly business review

### Compliance Co-Pilot — $40,000 setup + $4,000/month

Our most common engagement. Designed for firms with 50-500 reps where the compliance team is structurally underwater.

- Compliance review queue triage (emails, social, marketing, OBAs, gifts)
- Branch audit prep
- Customer complaint intake and routing
- Integration with your archiving system (Smarsh / Global Relay / Cisco), compliance management system, and CRM
- Up to 25,000 review items per month
- 12-week build
- 12-month minimum term
- Dedicated improvement sprint quarterly
- Direct line to the engineer who built your agent — no support tier

### Rep Productivity Suite — $60,000 setup + $6,000/month

For growth-mode BDs trying to attract reps from wirehouses with superior operational support.

- New account opening assistant
- Suitability documentation generator
- Reg BI documentation assistant
- Rep CE tracker
- Integration with up to seven systems including CRM, custodian platforms (Pershing, NFS, SEI), and your firm's policy library
- Unlimited interactions
- 16-week build
- 12-month minimum term
- On-call retainer hours included
- Priority support, 4-hour business-day response

## Add-ons

- **Additional integration** — $5,000-$10,000 one-time per system
- **Custom workflow** — $5,000-$15,000 one-time per workflow
- **Voice channel** — $1,000-$2,000/month
- **Higher interaction quota** — $0.10-$0.20 per interaction over your tier's quota

## How this compares to your alternatives

Most independent BDs evaluating us aren't trying to replace compliance staff — they're trying to extend what their existing team can take on without adding headcount. The traditional way to add that capacity is more bodies or more per-seat tooling:

- A senior compliance officer at an independent BD runs $120,000-$180,000 fully loaded.
- A compliance analyst runs $75,000-$110,000 fully loaded.
- A rep onboarding specialist runs $65,000-$95,000 fully loaded.
- Per-seat compliance tech (depending on the vendor) runs $200-$800/seat/month.

Our Compliance Co-Pilot at $40,000 setup plus $48,000 in retainer fees comes to **$88,000 in year one**, while extending the entire compliance team's capacity by 3-5x. Year two and beyond drops to $48,000. The math is meaningfully better than headcount, but the real point is that the people you already trust take on more of the work that actually needs them.

## What's not included

- Direct labor for tasks the agent cannot do (relationship-driven supervision, complex case management, regulator-facing communications)
- Major scope changes after the build (handled as add-ons or a new SOW)
- Custom integrations to systems we haven't worked with before (we'll quote these as add-ons)
- API/integration costs to third parties (e.g., your archiving vendor's API access fees)

## Payment terms

- Setup fee: 50% on signed SOW, 50% on go-live
- Monthly retainer: invoiced first of each month, net-30
- 90-day initial commitment, then 60-day notice to cancel month-to-month
- Volume pricing available for multi-vertical engagements (e.g., BD + RIA arm of the same parent)
