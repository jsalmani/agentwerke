# What Agentwerke Builds

Agentwerke builds custom Claude-based agents for small and mid-sized businesses. We focus on operational workflows — the repetitive, time-consuming office work that drains your team's attention and is hard to hire for.

## Core offering: the Office Manager Agent

Most of our clients start with an Office Manager Agent. It handles the day-to-day administrative work that a part-time office manager or executive assistant would normally do:

- **Inbox triage** — sorts incoming email by urgency, drafts replies for routine messages, escalates anything that needs a human.
- **Calendar coordination** — books meetings, reschedules, sends confirmations, manages buffers and prep time.
- **Vendor and customer follow-ups** — chases unpaid invoices, follows up on quotes, confirms appointments.
- **Document drafting** — generates first-draft proposals, contracts from templates, intake summaries, and meeting notes.
- **Intake and routing** — handles new customer or lead intake from your website, captures the right information, and routes it into your systems.
- **Reporting** — pulls weekly summaries from your CRM, calendar, and email so you walk into Monday with a one-page brief.

The agent runs inside your existing tools — Gmail or Outlook, Google Calendar or Microsoft 365, your CRM, and any other systems you grant it access to. We don't ask you to switch platforms. The agent works where your work already lives.

## Other agents we build

- **Customer support agents** — handle Tier 1 questions on your website, deflect ~40-60% of common tickets, hand off the rest to a human with full context.
- **Sales development agents** — research prospects, draft personalized outreach, qualify replies, and book discovery calls.
- **Internal knowledge agents** — answer team questions about your policies, processes, pricing, and product details, drawing from your actual docs.
- **Workflow automation agents** — handle multi-step processes that touch several systems (e.g., onboarding a new client across CRM, accounting, scheduling, and document signing).

## What we don't do

- **Generic chatbot widgets**. If you want a low-effort FAQ bot, you can use Chatbase or Intercom Fin and we'd recommend that.
- **Healthcare/PHI workflows**. We're not currently HIPAA-covered. We'll get there for the right client; we won't take the engagement until we are.
- **Anything that touches financial transactions or trades autonomously**. The agent can prepare and stage actions; a human approves and executes.
- **Voice agents** in the first version. We're text-only at launch; voice is a v2 conversation.
