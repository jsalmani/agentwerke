# What Agentwerke Builds

Agentwerke builds custom Claude-based agents that extend your existing team's capacity. We focus on operational workflows — the repetitive, time-consuming office work that's hard to hire for and that keeps your people from the work only they can do.

The framing matters: this isn't about replacing anyone. It's about the same humans, with leverage they didn't have before.

## Core offering: the Office Manager Agent

Most of our clients start with an Office Manager Agent. Paired with your existing team, it covers the day-to-day administrative work that would otherwise need a part-time office manager or executive assistant:

- **Inbox triage** — sorts incoming email by urgency, drafts replies for routine messages, escalates anything that needs a human.
- **Calendar coordination** — books meetings, reschedules, sends confirmations, manages buffers and prep time.
- **Vendor and customer follow-ups** — chases unpaid invoices, follows up on quotes, confirms appointments.
- **Document drafting** — generates first-draft proposals, contracts from templates, intake summaries, and meeting notes.
- **Intake and routing** — handles new customer or lead intake from your website, captures the right information, and routes it into your systems.
- **Reporting** — pulls weekly summaries from your CRM, calendar, and email so you walk into Monday with a one-page brief.

The agent does the heavy lifting on each of these; your people apply the judgment, hold the relationships, and approve anything that matters. It runs inside the tools you already use — Gmail or Outlook, Google Calendar or Microsoft 365, your CRM, and any other systems you grant it access to. We don't ask you to switch platforms.

## Other agents we build

- **Customer support agents** — extend your support team's coverage on Tier 1 questions, deflect ~40-60% of common tickets, hand the rest to a human with full context.
- **Sales development agents** — give your sellers a research and outreach drafting partner, qualify replies, and book discovery calls so reps can focus on the conversation.
- **Internal knowledge agents** — answer team questions about your policies, processes, pricing, and product details, drawing from your actual docs — so your subject-matter experts spend less time fielding the same questions.
- **Workflow automation agents** — handle multi-step processes that touch several systems (e.g., onboarding a new client across CRM, accounting, scheduling, and document signing), with humans approving the steps that need approval.

## What we don't do

- **Generic chatbot widgets**. If you want a low-effort FAQ bot, you can use Chatbase or Intercom Fin and we'd recommend that.
- **Healthcare/PHI workflows**. We're not currently HIPAA-covered. We'll get there for the right client; we won't take the engagement until we are.
- **Anything that touches financial transactions or trades autonomously**. The agent can prepare and stage actions; a human approves and executes.
- **Voice agents** in the first version. We're text-only at launch; voice is a v2 conversation.

## How an engagement works at a high level

We discover, scope, build, train, and hand off — typically in two weeks. Then we maintain and improve the agent monthly. See the Process and Pricing docs for detail.

The result, in plain terms: same team, more throughput, fewer of them stuck on work that doesn't need them.
