# Engagement Terms (Plain-English Summary)

This document summarizes the key terms of an Agentwerke engagement. The actual contract is our Master Services Agreement (MSA) with a Statement of Work (SOW) for your specific build. This summary is for orientation, not a substitute for the contract.

## Payment

- **Setup fee:** 50% on signed SOW, 50% on go-live (typically 14-21 days later).
- **Monthly retainer:** Invoiced first of each month, due net-15.
- **Net-30 available** for clients with 100+ employees on request.
- All fees in USD. Late payments accrue 1.5% per month.

## Term and termination

- **Initial term:** 90 days. The first 90 days establish the engagement and lock in your tier pricing.
- **After 90 days:** month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel.
- **For-cause termination:** either side can terminate for material breach with 30 days to cure.
- **On termination:** we export your conversation logs and operational data within 30 days. OAuth tokens are revoked. Your knowledge base content is yours; the agent's IP (system prompts, tool definitions, orchestration code) remains ours.

## Intellectual property

- **You own:** your data, your knowledge base content, your business logic, anything you brought to the engagement.
- **We own:** the agent itself — its system prompt, tool definitions, orchestration code, evaluation suites, and the methodologies we apply across clients.
- **You get:** a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the agent for your business operations during the term of the engagement.
- **Optional buyout:** after the initial 12-month term, you can buy out full ownership of the agent's IP for one year of retainer fees. This is rare; most clients value the ongoing maintenance more than the buyout.

## Confidentiality

- Mutual NDA covers everything either side learns about the other's business.
- Survives termination indefinitely for genuinely confidential information; 3 years for ordinary business information.
- We can reference the engagement publicly (e.g., "we built an agent for X") only with your written permission.

## Warranty and remedies

- We warrant the agent will perform substantially in accordance with the Agent Brief produced in discovery.
- If the agent fails to meet the brief, our remedy is to fix it or refund the setup fee. We don't disclaim this warranty.
- 30-day satisfaction guarantee on the setup fee. If the agent isn't doing what we agreed it would do at the end of the engagement, we keep working until it is, or we refund.

## Liability

- **Liability cap:** 12 months of fees paid (or 2x for healthcare or other regulated workloads).
- **Mutual indemnification** for IP claims, breach of confidentiality, and gross negligence.
- **Hallucination clause:** if the agent produces incorrect output and the receiving party failed to apply the human-review process specified in the Agent Brief, that's not on us. If the human-review process was followed and the agent still produced harm, our standard liability applies.
- **Anthropic dependency:** we disclaim liability for upstream model provider outages, pricing changes, or policy modifications. We retain the right to substitute an equivalent model with 30 days' notice.

## What's not covered

- Direct labor for tasks the agent cannot perform.
- Major scope changes (handled as Change Orders or new SOWs).
- Custom integrations to systems we haven't worked with before (quoted as add-ons).
- Third-party API costs you incur (e.g., your CRM's API fees).

## Service-level commitments

- Best-effort 99% uptime during business hours.
- Business-day response for non-critical issues.
- 4-hour response for production-down issues.
- One month of fees as service credit cap for sustained SLA breach.

## Insurance

We carry $1M/$3M Technology Errors & Omissions and $1M Cyber Liability with affirmative AI coverage. Certificates of Insurance available on request.

## Governing law

North Carolina, USA. Disputes resolved in Wake County state or federal courts unless both parties agree to arbitration.
