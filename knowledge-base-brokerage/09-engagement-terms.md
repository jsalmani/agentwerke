# Engagement Terms (Plain-English Summary)

This document summarizes the key terms of an Agentwerke broker-dealer engagement. The actual contract is our Master Services Agreement (MSA) with a Statement of Work (SOW) for your specific build, plus a Data Processing Addendum. This summary is for orientation, not a substitute for the contract.

## Payment

- **Setup fee:** 50% on signed SOW, 50% on go-live (typically 8-16 weeks after kickoff depending on tier).
- **Monthly retainer:** invoiced first of each month, due net-30.
- **Net-45 available** on request for firms with 100+ employees.
- All fees in USD. Late payments accrue 1.5% per month.

## Term and termination

- **Initial term:** 12 months. Tier pricing locks in for the initial term.
- **After 12 months:** auto-renews for 12-month terms with 60 days' notice to cancel.
- **For-cause termination:** either side can terminate for material breach with 30 days to cure.
- **On termination:** we export your conversation logs and operational data within 30 days in your archiving vendor's preferred format. OAuth tokens are revoked. Your knowledge base content is yours; the agent's IP (system prompts, tool definitions, orchestration code) remains ours unless you've exercised the buyout option.

## Intellectual property

- **You own:** your data, your knowledge base content, your WSPs and policies, your business logic, anything you brought to the engagement.
- **We own:** the agent itself — system prompt, tool definitions, orchestration code, evaluation suites, and the methodologies we apply across clients.
- **You get:** a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the agent for your business operations during the term.
- **Optional buyout:** after the initial 12-month term, you can buy out full ownership of the agent's IP for one year of retainer fees. This is rare; most clients value the ongoing maintenance and regulatory-update service more than the buyout.

## Confidentiality

- Mutual NDA covering everything either side learns about the other's business.
- Survives termination indefinitely for genuinely confidential information; 5 years for ordinary business information (longer than our parent agency terms because of the regulated nature of BD data).
- We can reference the engagement publicly (e.g., "we built a Compliance Co-Pilot for X") only with your written permission.

## Warranty and remedies

- We warrant the agent will perform substantially in accordance with the Agent Brief produced in discovery.
- If the agent fails to meet the brief, our remedy is to fix it or refund the setup fee.
- 30-day satisfaction guarantee on the setup fee. If the agent isn't doing what we agreed it would do at the end of the engagement, we keep working until it is, or we refund.
- We do not warrant against regulatory determinations by FINRA, the SEC, or state regulators — those determinations are inherently judgment calls beyond our control.

## Liability

- **Liability cap:** 12 months of fees paid (or 24 months for engagements involving direct customer-facing agent interactions, when we agree to those).
- **Mutual indemnification** for IP claims, breach of confidentiality, and gross negligence.
- **Compliance responsibility:** ultimate responsibility for regulatory compliance stays with the firm and its CCO. We provide tools that extend supervisory capacity; we are not a substitute for supervision.
- **Hallucination clause:** if the agent produces incorrect output and the firm failed to apply the human-review process specified in the Agent Brief, that's not on us. If the human-review process was followed and the agent still produced harm, our standard liability applies.
- **Anthropic dependency:** we disclaim liability for upstream model provider outages, pricing changes, or policy modifications. We retain the right to substitute an equivalent model with 30 days' notice.

## What's not covered

- Direct labor for tasks the agent cannot perform
- Major scope changes (handled as Change Orders or new SOWs)
- Custom integrations to systems we haven't worked with before (quoted as add-ons)
- Third-party API costs you incur (e.g., your archiving vendor's API access fees)
- FINRA examination defense or regulator-facing communications (these are services your law firm and compliance counsel provide, not us)

## Service-level commitments

- 99% uptime during business hours
- Business-day response for non-critical issues
- 4-hour response for production-down issues
- One month of fees as service credit cap for sustained SLA breach

## Insurance

We carry $1M/$3M Technology Errors & Omissions policy with affirmative AI coverage and $1M Cyber Liability. Certificates of Insurance available on request. We can name the firm as additional insured per engagement at no charge.

## Governing law

North Carolina, USA. Disputes resolved in Wake County state or federal courts unless both parties agree to arbitration. We do not require waiver of FINRA arbitration rights for any dispute the firm or its representatives might bring.
