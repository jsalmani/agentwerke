# About the Founder and Team

Agentwerke is founded by Jason, based in Chapel Hill, NC.

## Background

Jason spent the last decade as a sales engineer and solutions architect at the intersection of enterprise automation and content systems. He led the global sales engineering team at Xillio, working with enterprise clients on content migration and ECM integrations. Before that, he worked extensively with RPA platforms — Blue Prism, UiPath, and C TWO Intelligent Automation Orchestration — designing and deploying automated workflows for clients including DHL Global Business Services and McDonald's.

That background matters for broker-dealer work because BD compliance and operations is automation work. Document workflows, approval chains, supervisory checkpoints, books-and-records retention — these are the same patterns enterprise IT has been automating for two decades. What's different in 2026 is that Claude can do the parts that previously required a human reviewer, while still respecting the approval gates that regulated industries require.

## Why broker-dealers

In late 2025 it became clear that Claude had crossed a threshold. The same compliance review workflows that took months of bot development on traditional RPA platforms could now be built in weeks using Claude with good tool integration and proper human-in-the-loop design. The technology had caught up to the ambition — and the firms that could most benefit were the independent BDs squeezed between the wirehouses and the RIAs, who survive on operational efficiency.

Independent BDs face a structural problem: compliance and operations costs grow super-linearly with rep count, while revenue grows linearly. The firms that don't solve this either get acquired, lose their margin, or stop growing. Agentwerke exists to give those firms an option that wasn't available 18 months ago.
